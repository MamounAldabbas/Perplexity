About

My background

I’m a detective based in Washington with two decades of experience in the field. Over the years, I’ve worked on more than 100 cases and developed a methodical approach to solving difficult investigations.

I graduated from California State University with a criminology degree, which gave me a strong academic foundation for understanding criminal behavior, evidence, and procedure.

My approach

I focus on facts, patterns, and the details others miss. Every case deserves patience, structure, and a strategy that adapts as new evidence appears.
